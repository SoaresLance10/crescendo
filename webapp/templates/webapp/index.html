About Us

The raison d’etre of the Students' Council is to be a formal communication channel between the students, faculties and the administration. While it seeks to address any queries and resolve issues that the students and / or admin may face, the Student Council also relentlessly strives towards the enrichment of the campus experience.

As a team, we are 21 players with one heartbeat who take pride in being the voice and strength of a community of 1000+ students. We don't look for a reason to help others. We work for a cause, not for applause.
